About Me

Dr. K. Srinivasa Rao, M.Sc., M.Phil., Ph.D., has been working as a professor in the Department of Mathematics, I year coordinator, HOD of Basic Science and Humanities, Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering, Bengaluru, rich experience of teaching Mathematics in degree and Engineering Colleges, He received his bachelor's and master's degree from Bengaluru University. He was awarded Ph.D. degree from Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) in 2017 in the field of Graph Theory. He published more than 10 research articles in reputed international journals. He also presented his research articles in National and International Conferences. Dr. K. Srinivasa Rao Guiding 2 students in the field of Graph coloring. Published 1 patent in the year 2021-22. Attended conferences as resource person and chair.
